Key Insight
1. Campaign Channel performance
The analysis of conversion rates across different campaign channels revealed significant differences in performance.
Findings:
. Some channels consistently achieved higher conversion rates than others.
. High-performing channels indicate better audience targeting and engagement.
. Low-performing channels may require optimization or budget reduction.
. Business Impact

2. Campaign Type Effectiveness
Different campaign types produced varying levels of customer response.
Findings:
. Certain campaign types generated significantly higher conversion rates.
. Promotional and personalized campaigns generally perform better than generic campaigns.
. Campaign strategy influences customer engagement and purchasing behavior.

3. Advertising Spend Does Not Guarantee Success
The scatter plot between Ad Spend and Conversion Rate showed that higher spending does not always lead to better performance.
Findings:
. Some campaigns with moderate budgets achieved high conversion rates.
. Some expensive campaigns performed poorly.

4. Marketing Funnel Analysis
Marketing funnel stages:
Website Visit
      ↓
Email Open
      ↓
Email Click
      ↓
Conversion
Findings:
. Customer drop-off occurs at multiple stages.
. Funnel analysis helps identify weak points in customer journeys.
